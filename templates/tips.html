Tips

  • When attempting to build muscle, try and get around 1.2-2.0 grams of protein per body weight in kg per day.

  • Don't be scared of eating a healthy amount of fats; this macronutrient is an important and required part of your diet.

  • When strength training, try to ensure you are lifting with good form before adding more weight to the bar/machine.

  • Try to eat around 250-500 calories more than your maintenance calories when bulking.
